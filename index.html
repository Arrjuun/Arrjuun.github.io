Projects

Sketchers Grid

Drawing grid application for beginner artists.

New

An application to help beginner drawing artists who are focused on portrait drawing by overlaying a grid on their selected image. The grid can be resized, rotated and moved around to fit it with EYE units reference that is mainly used for portrait drawings. Also helpful in the case of other drawings. The images can be saved with or without grid. The saved image can be viewed without the screen turning off, in the app, for uninterrupted drawing experience.

Swing To Bin

A simple physics based pot the ball game.

New

A physics based simple puzzle game to understand the suspension, swinging, gravity, and collision. Cut the chain at the right moment to make the connected ball fall directly into the bin placed on the floor or by calculated bouncing behavior. The speed of the game can be varied which changes the gravity that is applied on the ball. Change speed, mute audio and check your stats in the settings.

Word Recognition

POC integrating TensorflowSharp in Unity with tensorflow sample. The word recognition model is based on the Simple Audio Recogniton tutorial by Tensorflow. It is based on the CNN for Small-footprint Keyword Spotting. The words that can be recognised are "yes", "no", "up", "down", "left", "right", "on", "off", "stop", and "go".
